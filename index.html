Tips & Tricks

Professional shortcuts and workflow optimizations

Keyboard Shortcuts

Photoshop

  • Ctrl+J - Duplicate Layer
  • Ctrl+T - Transform
  • Ctrl+Shift+I - Invert Selection
  • Ctrl+Alt+Z - Step Backward

Premiere Pro

  • C - Razor Tool
  • V - Selection Tool
  • Space - Play/Pause
  • Ctrl+K - Cut at Playhead
